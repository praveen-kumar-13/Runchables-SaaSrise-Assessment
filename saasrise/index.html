VIDEO: WHY SAASRISE WAS CREATED

Ryan Allis CEO and Founder of SaaSRise, the #1 SaaS Growth Community
Welcome to SaasRise, the #1 SaaS Growth community for CEOs and Founders with $1M-$100M in ARR.
If you're here, you probably got a special invitation from one of us or heard about us from a member.
This is something special, something authentic, something different -- a community just for SaaS CEOs and founders with $1M-$100M in ARR who are focused on rapid growth and scaling.
I co-founded and built iContact as CEO and sold it for $169 million after ten years of work to a publicly traded company, after scaling to $50M in ARR.
Since our exit, I've been a SaaS coach and community builder. I've coached the CEOs of Instantly, Seamless.ai, Tatango, Clearstream, YouCanBookMe, Green Packet, Retreaver, RXNT, RNL, and many more, helping them scale up from $3M to $160M+ in ARR.
Because of what I went through building iContact, I knew a community like this was needed -- and that it didn't yet exist. So we decided to create it.
